Spectrum Bands

Parag Kar
Apr 9, 2014

In India spectrum has been assigned to operators in 6 bands for exclusive use (licensed) as listed under. 800 MHz band: 824 to 844 MHz paired with 869 to 889 MHz. 900 MHz band: 890 to 915 MHz paired with 935 to 960 MHz. 1800 MHz band: 1710 to 1785 MHz paired with 1805 to 1880 MHz. 2100 MHz band: 1920 to 1980 MHz paired with 2110 to 2170 MHz.
