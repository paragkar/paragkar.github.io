Band Price

Parag Kar
Apr 9, 2014

Since 2010, the spectrum in licensed bands is being assigned through a transparent process of auctions. Therefore, every auction has a reserve price and a market-determined price (emanating out of the auction process). In general, these prices in have increased over the years, and are listed in the table titled “Actual Price“. However, since all auctions did not include all circles. Hence, the market prices for a particular service area can be deemed as those discovered in the last auction.
